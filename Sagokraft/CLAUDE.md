# Sagokraft — Product Context

## What it is

A Swedish children's reading companion (ages 4-8) that generates pedagogically
grounded, AI-rendered stories adapting to the child over time. Stories are the
medium; reading development is the outcome.

NOT an entertainment app, edutainment game, or content library.

## Target users

- **Primary buyer:** Swedish parents of children aged 4-8, concerned with
  literacy quality
- **End user:** The child (4-8), but they never interact with AI directly
- **Secondary channel:** Preschools and libraries (institutional trust strategy)

## Strategic positioning

Between institutional curriculum tools and mass-market reading apps.
Pedagogical but parent-facing. Adaptive but non-intrusive. AI-powered but
invisible. Calm and intentional — Scandinavian picture-book aesthetic.

## What makes it hard to copy

1. **Canonical blueprints with adaptive rendering** — plot and characters are
   curated; only language difficulty adapts. High editorial control,
   institution-ready.
2. **Longitudinal skill modeling WITHOUT testing** — reading profile built from
   passive signals (pacing, completion, audio use). Never tests or labels the
   child.
3. **Persistent characters as scaffold** — emotional continuity across stories
   raises switching costs and attachment.

## Key terminology

- **Blueprint** — curated narrative structure (fixed plot, characters, emotional
  beats); NOT a template
- **Rendering** — AI adjustment of sentence length, vocabulary, scaffolding
  within a blueprint
- **Bounded child agency** — child chooses characters/branches but cannot
  influence difficulty, structure, or session length
- **L2 story engine** — the AI layer that renders stories from blueprints
- **Reading profile** — passive skill model built over time, never shown to child

## Non-negotiables

- No quizzes, scores, levels, streaks, ads, or attention-driven mechanics
- No unconstrained story generation, social features, or user-generated content
- No autoplay
- AI is never visible to the child, never conversational, never framed as a
  feature
- Do not treat this as a content library or media app
- Do not conflate "adaptive" with "AI-generated freely" — blueprints constrain
  generation
- Do not apply Selftaped terminology, personas, or patterns here

## Business model

B2C subscription (annual/school-year framing). Limited free tier. Institutional
pilots (preschools, libraries) run in parallel as trust and defensibility
strategy. No IAP, no ad tier.

## Platform

Mobile app (iOS + Android). Built via Lovable / Supabase backend.

## Long-term ambition

Sweden's trusted early literacy companion. Expand 4-8 beachhead to ages 2-10,
multilingual support, deep institutional integration. Defensibility through
data, curated content, emotional attachment, and institutional trust.
